Excel to MySQL:

Analytic Techniques for Business

The specialization course: Excel to MySQL: Analytic Techniques for Business is offered by Duke University in the Coursera platform. The specialization consists of 4 courses and a final project that aims to build a good foundation for analytic techniques for business. The present serves the purpose of showing a reference of the skills acquired from the specialization.

Final Dashboard Of The Project.

Better decision-making for business. The importance of data.

The first course titled “Business Metrics for Data-Driven Companies” presents a good introduction to the work of a Business Analyst and a Data Analyst, highlighting the importance of stakeholders, requirements, data and the tools used to manipulate it for better decision-making with the objective of increasing profitability. I learned the importance of metrics and key business concepts such as cash-flow, revenue, profit, etc., also the influence and definitive impact of a decision or process based on data which can drive a business towards success.

More than a spreadsheet.The use of Excel for Data analysis.

The skills acquired in the second course “Mastering Data Analysis in Excel” can be divided in two aspects: The theory behind data analysis and the capabilities of Excel for data analysis. For data analysis theory I learned about binary classification, ROC curve, correlation, standardization, . All this theory can be applied in Excel for different business cases. The course provided guidance on how to use Excel for efficient data analysis.

The power of visualizations and Tableau. Conveying results and analysis.

The course “Data Visualization and Communication with Tableau” focus on the importance of sharing the insights obtained from the analysis with visualizations that are easier to understand than data. To achieve this the course gives guidance on the use of the software Tableau which specializes in visualizations.

SQL and MySQL. And how to do queries.

The course “Managing Big Data with MySQL” teaches the basics of the query language SQL and the relational database management system MySQL with the purpose of giving the students the skill to query the data they need in any given context. The reason behind this is because a Data analyst who is able to obtain the specific data required to do the analysis .

A final project.From requirements to presentation.

In the final project “Increasing Real Estate Management Profits: Harnessing Data Analytics” all skills learned and practiced are used in the context of a business case in real state management, that requires the analyst to go through all the processes of a project to convey a recommendation to the stakeholders; from gathering requirements, query only the useful data for the project, apply basic analysis using Excel, create an own methodology to determine which properties are profitable, implement the suggested visualizations and finally do a presentation to show the results.
